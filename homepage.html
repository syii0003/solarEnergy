Solar Estimator



Features of the estimator

  • Calculates your roofsize
  • Calculates the number of solar panels that can fit the roofsize
  • Know the nearest weather station
  • Get the recommendation of the solar panel system
  • Calculates the energy month-wise
  • Calcultes the environmental benefits


How to run the estimator?

  • Enter the home address
  • Draw the polygon on the rooftop of your house
  • Choose the number of residents and the day usage of electricity
  • Not happy with our recommendations? Choose your own solar panel type and calculate the energy.
