Focus & Goal

1. Our website provides a solar calculator to help you solve the solar panel installation issues
2. We provide the rooftop estimator and give the most suitable recommendations, as well as relavant information to help you in deciding whether to invest in solar panels or not.
3. We aim to promote sustainability in Victoria and help to develop an environmental-friendly society.

Our target

We welcome everyone who wants to install the solar panels. Our website is designed for different types of homeowners such as office workers, parents with kids and elderly people. The website will help you find the best solar solution, which customizes you solar panel calculating service and reflects your contribution to the environment.

Our motivation


1. Australia heavily relies on the non-renewable sources of energy such as coal for the generation of electricity.
2. Using coal generated electricty causes a serious threat to the environment in land, air and water pollution.
